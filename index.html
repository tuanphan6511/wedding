Our Story

How We Met

On a rainy afternoon in a small bookstore café, NLoan was lost in her favorite novel when NTuấn accidentally knocked over her coffee. What started as an embarrassing moment turned into hours of conversation about literature, dreams, and the magic of serendipity.

The Proposal

Two years later, NTuấn returned to that same café. As NLoan opened what she thought was a new book, she found pages filled with their memories and a simple question: "Will you write the rest of our story together?" The ring was hidden in the bookmark.
