How We Met

We first crossed paths at a cozy coffee shop in downtown Seattle on a rainy Tuesday morning. Ngọc Loan was reading her favorite book, and Ngọc Tuấn couldn't help but notice the beautiful smile that lit up her face with every page. What started as a conversation about literature turned into hours of talking about dreams, travel, and life.

The Proposal

On a breathtaking sunset hike at Mount Rainier, Ngọc Tuấn got down on one knee at the very spot where we had our first anniversary picnic. With the golden light painting the mountains and Ngọc Loan's favorite song playing softly in the background, he asked her to be his forever adventure partner.
