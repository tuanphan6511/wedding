Our Story

How We Met

Our paths crossed on a quiet Sunday morning at a local bookstore. Ngọc Loan was lost in the pages of her favorite novel, while Ngọc Tuấn was searching for inspiration in the poetry section. A shared smile over coffee led to hours of conversation about dreams, literature, and life.

The Proposal

Under the golden light of a sunset at our favorite hiking spot, Ngọc Tuấn asked Ngọc Loan to be his forever companion. With the mountains as witnesses and tears of joy, she said yes to a lifetime of adventures together.
